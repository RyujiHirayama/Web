Volumetric display containing multiple two-dimensional color motion pictures

Ryuji Hirayama, Hirotaka Nakayama, Atsushi Shiraki, Takashi Kakue, Tomoyoshi Shimobaba, and Tomoyoshi Ito,
SPIE DSS 2014, Baltimore, USA (May 6-8, 2014). [link]


Abstract

We made a volumetric display consisting of 7 x 7 threads and a projector. The threads are arranged to avoid the overlap from the viewpoint of the projector when illuminated so that the scattering from each of the threads can be controlled independently. Thereby, an arbitrary dynamic full-color 3D image can be rendered on the threads. We demonstrate that this thread-based volumetric display can exhibit two dynamic full-color images in different orthogonal directions.
