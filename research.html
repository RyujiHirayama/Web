Purpose

My research interests include 3D display technology and its applications as human-computer interaction. In particular, I have been focusing on a unique 3D structure which contains multiple 2D images. Each of the images contained in the 3D structure can be obtained only from a prescribed viewpoint. Exploiting such steganographic feature of our 3D structure, I aim to develop novel information service systems, which can be applied to digital signage, media art, amusement, and security. For this reason, I am currently working mainly on the improvement of the modeling method of the 3D structure and the development of a new type of volumetric display.
